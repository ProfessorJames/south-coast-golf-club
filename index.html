South Coast Golf Club

#1 Golf Destination on the South Coast of Ireland – Golf Digest 2023

Welcome to South Coast Golf Club, a masterpiece crafted by the renowned golf architect, Tom Fazio. Situated along the breathtaking South Coast of Ireland, our course seamlessly combines natural beauty with the artistry of Fazio's design.

Carved into the coastal landscape, our course offers challenging fairways and scenic vistas. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or a beginner, you'll find a rewarding golfing experience here.

With a history dating back to 1916, South Coast Golf Club is a place where generations of golfers have made memories. Join us and become part of this storied tradition. Discover the magic of golf along the South Coast at South Coast Golf Club!

Our tradition of warm and genuine hospitality has been woven into the very fabric of this golfing haven. The South Coast Golf Club was conceived with the aim of not just impressing but enchanting our guests. This tradition comes alive in our meticulously restored Clubhouse, adorned with timeless elegance and attention to every detail. Our dedicated staff, who provide personalized and intimate service, are the heart and soul of our establishment.

As you explore our lush 840-acre golfing paradise, indulge in culinary excellence at our acclaimed restaurant, navigate our stunning golf course, and embrace the serenity of our spa, you will uncover the essence of the South Coast Golf Club. We offer prestige without pretension and an enchanting experience without any unnecessary frills. Above all, we offer a profound sense of belonging. Here, you are not just a member, you are part of our family, and this is your golfing home.

Benefits of Membership

Joining South Coast Golf Club can offer a range of benefits for golf enthusiasts. These are some of the many benefits you can expect to enjoy when you join South Coast Golf Club:

  • Scenic golf course and exclusive tee time access.
  • Opportunities for improving your golf skills.
  • Social networking and camaraderie with fellow members.
  • Access to clubhouse amenities and member-only events.
  • Professional golf lessons and discounts on golf-related items.
  • A supportive and engaged golfing community.
